Producer Mag is an initiative started by electronic musician and producer Joy Bhardwaj aka soundsofJOY_ who makes atmospheric and glitch infused dance music and his friend Alrick Lobo who specializes in making different styles and variants of house music. The main aim of this initiative is to promote and popularize all kinds of electronic music genres and artists who make them.
